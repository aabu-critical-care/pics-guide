What is PICS?

Definition: PICS refers to new or worsening impairments in physical, cognitive, or mental health status arising after critical illness.

Impact: 50–70% of ICU survivors are affected.

Core Components:

Physical Cognitive Psych

Physical Impairments

  • ICU-acquired weakness.
  • Reduced endurance.
  • Difficulty with daily activities.

Cognitive Impairments

  • Memory deficits.
  • Poor concentration.
  • Slower processing speed.

Psychological Impairments

  • Anxiety & Depression.
  • PTSD symptoms.

Management

Multidisciplinary approach: Early mobilization, Education, and Follow-up.
